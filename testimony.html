He instructed me to look for white handkerchief or cotton wool and put rubb on it that i should put it in my nose and inhale it, that it will stop. A few minutes later, the bleeding stopped. Children of God should help me shout hallelujah to appreciate God on my behalf.

3) Testimony of Deliverance From the spirit of Madness Sis Elizabeth, Lagos. Mon, 20th January 2025.
Dear Pastor, Calvary greetings to you and the entire members of this powerful ministry. I want to thank God of this commission for his deep love and concern over my life. It all started like a minor thing last year. I noticed I kept forgetting things easily . On the 7th of January this year, at times while walking on the road, I will hear a voice within me telling me to take off my clothes. This voice was so compelling, but for the grace of God, i was resisting it. But after some time, I almost took them off on the road. It happened again the second day. As I got to the gate of my house, I rushed into the compound and took off my clothes while climbing upstairs. Then, I sought the face of the Holy Spirit through our father in the Lord. The Holy Spirit said they had already sent the spirit of madness to me and just waiting for it's manifestation. Without having to fast nor involve myself in extensive spiritual exercise & prayers, a simple solution from the Holy Spirit was proffered. Holy Spirit said I should just stay indoors for three days!On the first day of my being indoors, he prayed and anointed oil through phone calls, instructing me to pour it over my head saying I will not run mad. On the third and final day, the Holy Spirit prayed again over the phone and anointed oil, which I poured from my head down and bathed immediately & I was relieved. A few days latter, after coming out, I started feeling my head expanding as if it wanted to burst. The pain was so excruciating that I had to call on pastor again. He said I should just claim the work rhe Holy Spirit had done. That it was just a sign to truly show me that they had in the spiritual realm made me mad and only waiting for the physical manifestation. After going through all those attacks, now I am ok. Praise God. Halleluyah. I praise and thank the God of SCCM for delivering me from insanity and putting the enemies to shame. Glory be to God of SCCM. God bless you, sir. Your anointing will never get dry in Jesus' mighty name. Agba yin o ni di agbaya, e dagba, e dogbo. Oke ti e ti wa, e ni suubu oo. Iwaju le o ma lo, e ni pada seyin o. God bless you, sir. May God continue to use you for us. You will never fall. Thank you, sir. Pls let the church help me to sing this song to praise my God. Thank you Lord..thank you Lord.. all I have to say is thank you, Lord.
